# Charm of Vitality


This Charm allows you to give yourself the benefit of a [Potion of Vitality](potion-of-vitality-xdmg.md) as a [Magic](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Magic) action. Once you do so, the Charm vanishes from you.

## Sources

*Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 99*
