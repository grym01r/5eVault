# Boon of Speed 


***Prerequisites*** 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Escape Artist.** As a Bonus Action, you can take the [Disengage](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Disengage) action, which also ends the [Grappled](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Grappled) condition on you.

**Quickness.** Your Speed increases by 30 feet.
