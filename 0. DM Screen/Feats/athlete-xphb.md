# Athlete 


***Prerequisites*** 4th; Strength 13 or higher or 4th; Dexterity 13 or higher

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Climb Speed.** You gain a Climb Speed equal to your Speed.

**Hop Up.** When you have the [Prone](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Prone) condition, you can right yourself with only 5 feet of movement.

**Jumping.** You can make a running Long or High Jump after moving only 5 feet.
