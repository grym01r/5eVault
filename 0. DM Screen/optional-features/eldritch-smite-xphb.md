# Eldritch Smite 


***Prerequisites*** Level 5, pact of the blade|xphb

Once per turn when you hit a creature with your pact weapon, you can expend a Pact Magic spell slot to deal an extra `1d8` Force damage to the target, plus another `1d8` per level of the spell slot, and you can give the target the [Prone](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Prone) condition if it is Huge or smaller.

*Player's Handbook (2024) p. 155*
