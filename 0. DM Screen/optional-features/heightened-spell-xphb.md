# Heightened Spell 


When you cast a spell that forces a creature to make a saving throw, you can spend 2 Sorcery Points to give one target of the spell [Disadvantage](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on saves against the spell.
