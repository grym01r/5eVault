# Evasive Footwork 


As a [Bonus Action](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can expend one Superiority Die and take the [Disengage](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Disengage) action. You also roll the die and add the number rolled to your AC until the start of your next turn.
## Sources

*Player's Handbook (2024) p. 95*
