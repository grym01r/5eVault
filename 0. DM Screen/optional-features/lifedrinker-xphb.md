# Lifedrinker 


***Prerequisites*** Level 9, pact of the blade|xphb

Once per turn when you hit a creature with your pact weapon, you can deal an extra `1d6` Necrotic, Psychic, or Radiant damage (your choice) to the creature, and you can expend one of your [Hit Point Dice](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md) to roll it and regain a number of [Hit Points](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the roll plus your Constitution modifier (minimum of 1 [Hit Point](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md)).
