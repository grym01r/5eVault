# Thirsting Blade 


***Prerequisites*** Level 5, pact of the blade|xphb

You gain the Extra Attack feature for your pact weapon only. With that feature, you can attack twice with the weapon instead of once when you take the [Attack](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Attack) action on your turn.
