# Boon of Truesight 


***Prerequisites*** 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Truesight.** You have [Truesight](0.%20DM%20Screen/senses.md#Truesight) with a range of 60 feet.
