# Boon of Dimensional Travel 


***Prerequisites*** 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Blink Steps.** Immediately after you take the [Attack](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Attack) action or the [Magic](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Magic) action, you can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see.
