# Truth Serum
*Adventuring gear, poison (ingested)*  

- **Properties**: [Poison](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 150 gp

A creature subjected to Truth Serum must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or have the [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 hour. The [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature can't knowingly communicate a lie.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 91. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span>*
