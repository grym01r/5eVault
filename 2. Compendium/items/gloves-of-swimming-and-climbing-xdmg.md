# Gloves of Swimming and Climbing
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  

While wearing these gloves, climbing and swimming don't cost you extra movement, and you gain a +5 bonus to Strength ([Athletics](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#Athletics)) checks made to climb or swim.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 265. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
