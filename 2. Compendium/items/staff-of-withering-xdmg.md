# Staff of Withering
*Staff, weapon, rare (requires attunement by a cleric, druid, or warlock)*  
![](staff-of-withering.webp#right)

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d6 bludgeoning
  - Two-handed: 1d8 bludgeoning
- **Properties**: [Versatile](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Versatile)
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

This staff has 3 charges and regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn.

The staff can be wielded as a magic Quarterstaff. On a hit, it deals damage as a normal Quarterstaff , and you can expend 1 charge to deal an extra `2d10` Necrotic damage to the target and force it to make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target has [Disadvantage](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) for 1 hour on any ability check or saving throw that uses Strength or Constitution.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 312. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
