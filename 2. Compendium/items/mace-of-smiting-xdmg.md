# Mace of Smiting
*Weapon ([mace](mace-xphb.md)), rare*  
![](mace-of-smiting.webp#right)

- **Damage**: 1d6 bludgeoning
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The bonus increases to +3 when you use the weapon to attack a Construct.

When you roll a 20 on an attack roll made with this weapon, the target takes an extra 7 Bludgeoning damage, or 14 Bludgeoning damage if it's a Construct. If a Construct has 25 [Hit Points](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or fewer after taking this damage, it is destroyed.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 276. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
