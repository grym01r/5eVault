# Sword of Life Stealing
*Rare (requires attunement)*  
![](sword-of-life-stealing.webp#right)

When you attack a creature with this magic weapon and roll a 20 on the `d20` for the attack roll, that target takes an extra 15 Necrotic damage if it isn't a Construct or an Undead, and you gain [Temporary Hit Points](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the amount of Necrotic damage taken.
