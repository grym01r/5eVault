# Torch
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 1 cp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

A Torch burns for 1 hour, casting [Bright Light](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 20-foot radius and [Dim Light](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 20 feet. When you take the [Attack](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Attack) action, you can attack with the Torch, using it as a Simple Melee weapon. On a hit, the target takes 1 Fire damage.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 229. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
