# Scimitar of Speed
*Weapon ([scimitar](scimitar-xphb.md)), very rare (requires attunement)*  
![](scimitar-of-speed.webp#right)

- **Damage**: 1d6 slashing
- **Properties**: [Finesse](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Light](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Light)
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, you can make one attack with it as a [Bonus Action](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) on each of your turns.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 302. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
