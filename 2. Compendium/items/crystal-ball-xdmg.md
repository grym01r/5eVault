# Crystal Ball
*Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

The typical crystal ball, a very rare item, is about 6 inches in diameter. While touching it, you can cast the [scrying](scrying-xphb.md) spell (save DC 17) with it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 245. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
