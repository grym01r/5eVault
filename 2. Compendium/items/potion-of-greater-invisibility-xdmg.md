# Potion of Greater Invisibility
*Potion, very rare*  
![](potion-of-greater-invisibility.webp#right)

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

This potion's container looks empty but feels as though it holds liquid. When you drink the potion, you have the [Invisible](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Invisible) condition for 1 hour.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 288*
