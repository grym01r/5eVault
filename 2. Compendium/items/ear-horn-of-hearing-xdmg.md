# Ear Horn of Hearing
*Wondrous item, common*  

While held up to your ear, this horn suppresses the effects of the [Deafened](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Deafened) condition on you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 256*
