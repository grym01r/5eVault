# Lamp
*Adventuring gear*  
![](lamp.webp#right)

- **Cost**: 5 sp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

A Lamp burns [Oil](oil-xphb.md) as fuel to cast [Bright Light](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 15-foot radius and [Dim Light](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 30 feet.
