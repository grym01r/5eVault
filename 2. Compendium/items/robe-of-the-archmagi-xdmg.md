# Robe of the Archmagi
*Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement by a sorcerer, warlock, or wizard)*  
![](robe-of-the-archmagi.webp#right)

This elegant garment is made from exquisite cloth and adorned with runes.

You gain these benefits while wearing the robe.

## Armor

If you aren't wearing armor, your base [Armor Class](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) is 15 plus your Dexterity modifier.

## Magic Resistance

You have [Advantage](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

## War Mage

Your spell save DC and spell attack bonus each increase by 2.
