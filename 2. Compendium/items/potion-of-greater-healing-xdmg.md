# Potion of Greater Healing
*Potion, uncommon*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

You regain `4d4 + 4` [Hit Points](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) when you drink this potion. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 288. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
