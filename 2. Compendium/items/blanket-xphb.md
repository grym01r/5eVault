# Blanket
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 5 sp
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

While wrapped in a blanket, you have [Advantage](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws against [extreme cold](extreme-cold-xdmg.md) (see the "Dungeon Master's Guide").
