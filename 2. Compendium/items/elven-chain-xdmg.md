# Elven Chain
*Rare*  
![](elven-chain.webp#right)

You gain a +1 bonus to [Armor Class](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) while you wear this armor. You are considered trained with this armor even if you lack training with Medium or Heavy armor.

**Variants**:
- [Elven Chain Mail](#Elven%20Chain%20Mail)
- [Elven Chain Shirt](#Elven%20Chain%20Shirt)

### Elven Chain Mail

- **Armor Class**: 16
- **Strength**: Requires 13 STR.
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Weight**: 55.0 lbs.

### Elven Chain Shirt

- **Armor Class**: 13 + Dex modifier (max of +2)
- **Weight**: 20.0 lbs.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 257. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
