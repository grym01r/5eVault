# Carrion Crawler Mucus
*Adventuring gear, poison (contact)*  

- **Properties**: [Poison](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 200 gp

A creature subjected to Carrion Crawler Mucus must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 minute. The creature also has the [Paralyzed](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Paralyzed) condition while [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way. The creature repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 90. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> (as "Crawler Mucus (Contact)")*
