# Ring of Resistance
*Ring, rare*  

You have [Resistance](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to one damage type while wearing this ring. The gemstone in the ring indicates the type, which the DM chooses or determines randomly by rolling on the following table.

| 3 | [Fire](ring-of-fire-resistance-xdmg.md) | Garnet |
