# Potion of Mind Reading
*Potion, rare*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [Detect Thoughts](detect-thoughts-xphb.md) spell (save DC 13) for 10 minutes (no [Concentration](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Concentration) required).

This potion's dense, purple liquid has an ovoid cloud of pink floating in it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 288. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
