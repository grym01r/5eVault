# Executioner's Axe
*Very rare*  

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

Any Humanoid you hit with the weapon takes an extra `2d6` Slashing damage, and you gain [Temporary Hit Points](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the extra damage dealt.

**Variants**:
- [Executioner's Battleaxe](#Executioner's%20Battleaxe)
- [Executioner's Greataxe](#Executioner's%20Greataxe)
- [Executioner's Halberd](#Executioner's%20Halberd)
- [Executioner's Handaxe](#Executioner's%20Handaxe)

### Executioner's Battleaxe

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d8 slashing
  - Two-handed: 1d10 slashing
- **Properties**: [Versatile](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Versatile)
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

### Executioner's Greataxe

- **Damage**: 1d12 slashing
- **Properties**: [Heavy](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Heavy), [Two-Handed](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Two-Handed)
- **Weight**: 7.0 lbs.

### Executioner's Halberd

- **Damage**: 1d10 slashing
- **Properties**: [Heavy](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Heavy), [Reach](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Reach), [Two-Handed](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Two-Handed)

### Executioner's Handaxe

- **Damage**: 1d6 slashing
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Light](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Light), [Thrown](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Thrown)
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.
