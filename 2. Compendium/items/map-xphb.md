# Map
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 1 gp

If you consult an accurate Map, you gain a +5 bonus to Wisdom ([Survival](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#Survival)) checks you make to find your way in the place represented on it.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 227. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
