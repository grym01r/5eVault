# Oil of Taggit
*Adventuring gear, poison (contact)*  

- **Properties**: [Poison](0.%20DM%20Screen/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 400 gp

A creature subjected to Oil of Taggit must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 24 hours. The creature also has the [Unconscious](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Unconscious) condition while [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way. It wakes up if it takes damage.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 91. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span>*
