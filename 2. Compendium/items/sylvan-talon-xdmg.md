# Sylvan Talon
*Common (requires attunement)*  
![](sylvan-talon.webp#right)

While this weapon is on your person, you understand the non-written communication of all Fey, and they understand yours.

## Secret Message

As a [Magic](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Magic) action, you can use the weapon to cast [Message](message-xphb.md). Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.
