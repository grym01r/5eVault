# Costume
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 5 gp
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

While wearing a Costume, you have [Advantage](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on any ability check you make to impersonate the person or type of person it represents.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 225. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
