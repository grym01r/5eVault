# Mithral Armor
*Uncommon*  

Mithral is a light, flexible metal. A mithral chain shirt or breastplate can be worn under normal clothes.

If the armor normally imposes disadvantage on Dexterity ([Stealth](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#Stealth)) checks or has a Strength requirement, the mithral version of the armor doesn't.
