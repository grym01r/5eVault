# Protection from Poison
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 hour

You touch a creature and end the [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition on it. For the duration, the target has [Advantage](advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws to avoid or end the [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition, and it has [Resistance](0.%20DM%20Screen/Variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Poison damage.
