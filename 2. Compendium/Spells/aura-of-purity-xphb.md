# Aura of Purity
*4th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot [Emanation](emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) for the duration. While in the aura, you and your allies have [Resistance](0.%20DM%20Screen/Variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Poison damage and [Advantage](advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws to avoid or end effects that include the [Blinded](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Blinded), [Charmed](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Charmed), [Deafened](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Deafened), [Frightened](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Frightened), [Paralyzed](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Paralyzed), [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned), or [Stunned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Stunned) condition.
