# Mass Heal
*9th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A flood of healing energy flows from you into creatures around you. You restore up to 700 [Hit Points](hit-points-xphb.md), divided as you choose among any number of creatures that you can see within range. Creatures healed by this spell also have the [Blinded](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Deafened), and [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned) conditions removed from them.
