# Protection from Energy
*3rd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has [Resistance](0.%20DM%20Screen/Variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to one damage type of your choice: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder.
