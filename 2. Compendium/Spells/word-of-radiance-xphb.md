# Word of Radiance
*cantrip, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (5-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, M (a sunburst token)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Burning radiance erupts from you in a 5-foot [Emanation](emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Each creature of your choice that you can see in it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take `1d6` Radiant damage.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `1d6` when you reach levels 5 (`2d6`), 11 (`3d6`), and 17 (`4d6`).
