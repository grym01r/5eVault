# Lesser Restoration
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and end one condition on it: [Blinded](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Deafened), [Paralyzed](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Paralyzed), or [Poisoned](0.%20DM%20Screen/conditions.md#Poisoned).
