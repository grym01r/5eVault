# Mass Healing Word
*3rd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range regain [Hit Points](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to `2d4` plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The healing increases by `1d4` for each spell slot level above 3.
