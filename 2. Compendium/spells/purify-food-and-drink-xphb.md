# Purify Food and Drink
*1st-level, Transmutation (ritual)*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action unless cast as a ritual
- **Range:** 10 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You remove poison and rot from nonmagical food and drink in a 5-foot-radius [Sphere](0.%20DM%20Screen/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) centered on a point within range.
