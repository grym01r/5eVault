# Mangonel
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 97* 

![](mangonel.webp#right)

A Mangonel is a catapult that hurls heavy projectiles in a high arc, so it can hit targets behind walls. Loading a Mangonel requires two [Utilize](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Utilize) actions, and aiming it requires two more [Utilize](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Utilize) actions. Then a crew member can take the Mangonel Stone action.
