# Keg Launcher
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 96* 

![](keg-launcher.webp#right)

A back-mounted, wooden catapult flings small kegs of toxic gas. Loading a Keg Launcher requires the [Utilize](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Utilize) action, and aiming it requires another [Utilize](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Utilize) action. Then a crew member can take the Toxic Keg action.
