# Ballista
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 96* 

A Ballista is a massive crossbow that fires heavy bolts. Loading a Ballista requires the [Utilize](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Utilize) action, and aiming it requires another [Utilize](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Utilize) action. Then a crew member can take the Ballista Bolt action.
