# Brooch of Shielding
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](brooch-of-shielding.webp#right)

While wearing this brooch, you have [Resistance](0.%20DM%20Screen/Variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Force damage, and you have [Immunity](immunity-xphb.md) to damage from the [Magic Missile](magic-missile-xphb.md) spell.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 241. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
