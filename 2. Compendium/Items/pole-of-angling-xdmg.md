# Pole of Angling
*Wondrous item, common*  

This item functions as a Pole. While holding it, you can take a [Magic](0.%20DM%20Screen/actions.md#Magic) action to cause it to transform into a fishing pole with a hook, a line, and a reel, or have the fishing pole revert to a Pole.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 286*
