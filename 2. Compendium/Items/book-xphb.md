# Book
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 25 gp
- **Weight**: 5.0 lbs.

A Book contains fiction or nonfiction. If you consult an accurate nonfiction Book about its topic, you gain a +5 bonus to Intelligence ([Arcana](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#Arcana), [History](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#History), [Nature](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#Nature), or [Religion](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#Religion)) checks you make about that topic.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 224. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
