# Potion of Resistance
*Potion, uncommon*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

When you drink this potion, you have [Resistance](0.%20DM%20Screen/Variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to one type of damage for 1 hour. The DM chooses the type or determines it randomly by rolling on the following table.
