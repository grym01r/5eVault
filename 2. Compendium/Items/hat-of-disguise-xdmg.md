# Hat of Disguise
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](hat-of-disguise.webp#right)

While wearing this hat, you can cast the [Disguise Self](disguise-self-xphb.md) spell. The spell ends if the hat is removed.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 266. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
