# Cloak of Elvenkind
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](cloak-of-elvenkind.webp#right)

While you wear this cloak, Wisdom ([Perception](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#Perception)) checks made to perceive you have [Disadvantage](disadvantage-xphb.md), and you have [Advantage](advantage-xphb.md) on Dexterity ([Stealth](0.%20DM%20Screen/skills.md#Stealth)) checks.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 244. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
